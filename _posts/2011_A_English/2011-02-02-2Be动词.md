#### Be动词的形式

```text
be 
is am are
was were
being
been
```
```text
The man is back.
They are back.
He was back.
They were back.
They have been back.
```


#### Be动词的用法

后面接名词、形容词、地点副词或短语作补足语   
```text
The man is teacher.
Mary's new dresses are colorful.
My mother was in the kitchen.
```
