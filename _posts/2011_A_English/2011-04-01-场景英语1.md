## <a id="content1">场景内容</a>
What can I get you?  coffee ? Tea?        
<span style="color:grey;font-size:12px;">您想要点什么</span>

Milk and sugar?    
<span style="color:grey;font-size:12px;">牛奶和糖？</span>

No milk , please. But sugar      
<span style="color:grey;font-size:12px;">请不要牛奶。但是糖</span>

Would you like anything to eat?  A slice of chocolate cake…    some pastries?    
<span style="color:grey;font-size:12px;">你想吃点东西吗？一块巧克力蛋糕……一些糕点？</span>

Tempting, but no, thanks, just tea…     
<span style="color:grey;font-size:12px;">听起来很不错，不过算了，谢谢，就喝茶吧……</span>

Amazing!     
<span style="color:grey;font-size:12px;">太棒了！</span>

Who?  I mean what?     
<span style="color:grey;font-size:12px;">谁？我的意思是什么？</span>

Egypt  What an incredible place !     
<span style="color:grey;font-size:12px;">埃及 这是一个多么神奇的地方啊！</span>

Yes, it’s fascinating. Are you interested in travelling?      
<span style="color:grey;font-size:12px;">是的，这确实很有趣。你对旅行感兴趣吗？</span>

Oh yes! I love travelling. I haven’t got much time with the bar and everything…  but there are so many places I’d like to visit        
<span style="color:grey;font-size:12px;">哦，是的！我喜欢旅行。因为酒吧和其他事情，我没有太多时间……但是有很多地方我想去参观</span>

Do you travel a lot     
<span style="color:grey;font-size:12px;">你经常旅行吗</span>

Yes, I do     
<span style="color:grey;font-size:12px;">是的，我经常旅行</span>

Lucky you. Have you got a lot of free time?       
<span style="color:grey;font-size:12px;">你真幸运。你有很多空闲时间吗</span>

Not really, I travel for my job.     
<span style="color:grey;font-size:12px;">并非如此，我旅行是因工作需要。</span>

Oh wonderful!     
<span style="color:grey;font-size:12px;">哦，太棒了！</span>

What do you do ?     
<span style="color:grey;font-size:12px;">你是做什么工作的？</span>

I’m a photographer     
<span style="color:grey;font-size:12px;">我是一名摄影师</span>

Are you ? How interesting!  Is this one of your pictures then ?     
<span style="color:grey;font-size:12px;">你是吗？真有趣！那这是你的画作之一吗？</span>

No, it isn’t. But I know the photographer who took it     
<span style="color:grey;font-size:12px;">不，不是。但我认识拍这张照片的摄影师</span>

Do you work for a magazine?      
<span style="color:grey;font-size:12px;">你为杂志工作吗？</span>

Sometimes I work freelance.So, I work for different magazines and newspapers. But I also work for a publishing company. I take pictures for their travel books and guides    
<span style="color:grey;font-size:12px;">有时我做自由职业者。所以，我为不同的杂志和报纸工作。但我也为一家出版公司工作。我为他们的旅行书和指南拍照</span>

Wow! That sounds really interesting !  You probably like travelling too, then?       
<span style="color:grey;font-size:12px;">哇！那听起来真的很有趣！那你很可能也应该喜欢旅行吧？</span>

Yes, very much. In fact, I’m not often in London, I spend most of my time travelling! I’ve got a job to do in Chile. Some pictures for a magazine. I’m going to be there for a few months actually     
<span style="color:grey;font-size:12px;">是的，非常喜欢。事实上，我不常在伦敦，我大部分时间都在旅行！我在智利有一份工作。为杂志拍一些照片。实际上我要在那里待几个月</span>

Gosh! How I envy you!     
<span style="color:grey;font-size:12px;">天哪！我真羡慕你啊！</span>

Do you want to come with me ?      
<span style="color:grey;font-size:12px;">你想和我一起去吗？</span>

I wish!     
<span style="color:grey;font-size:12px;">我希望如此！</span>

So, why do you like travelling so much?     
<span style="color:grey;font-size:12px;">那么，你为什么这么喜欢旅行？</span>


Oh for a million reasons! First of all because it’s fun and interesting to see the way people live in other countries. And I think it’s important to have new experiences to expose yourself to other cultures…     
<span style="color:grey;font-size:12px;">有太多原因了。第一条看到其他国家人民的生活方式真是既有趣又令人好奇。而且我认为，拥有新的经历并接触其他文化是非常重要的</span>

Absolutely. I agree. How much do I owe you for the tea?       
<span style="color:grey;font-size:12px;">当然。我同意。我应该给您付多少钱的茶钱呢？</span>

Oh, nothing. It’s on the house     
<span style="color:grey;font-size:12px;">哦，没什么。这顿饭我来买单。</span>

Well, thanks…      
<span style="color:grey;font-size:12px;">嗯，谢谢……</span>

My name is Archie. Pleased to meet you      
<span style="color:grey;font-size:12px;">我叫阿尔奇。很高兴见到你</span>

Pleased to meet you, Archie      
<span style="color:grey;font-size:12px;">很高兴见到你，阿尔奇</span>
