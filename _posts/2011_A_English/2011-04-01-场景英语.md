## <a id="content1">场景一</a>
What can I get you?  coffee ? Tea?        
<span style="color:grey;font-size:12px;">您想要点什么</span>

Milk and sugar?    
<span style="color:grey;font-size:12px;">牛奶和糖？</span>

No milk , please. But sugar      
<span style="color:grey;font-size:12px;">请不要牛奶。但是糖</span>

Would you like anything to eat?  A slice of chocolate cake…    some pastries?    
<span style="color:grey;font-size:12px;">你想吃点东西吗？一块巧克力蛋糕……一些糕点？</span>

Tempting, but no, thanks, just tea…     
<span style="color:grey;font-size:12px;">听起来很不错，不过算了，谢谢，就喝茶吧……</span>

Amazing!     
<span style="color:grey;font-size:12px;">太棒了！</span>

Who?  I mean what?     
<span style="color:grey;font-size:12px;">谁？我的意思是什么？</span>

Egypt  What an incredible place !     
<span style="color:grey;font-size:12px;">埃及 这是一个多么神奇的地方啊！</span>

Yes, it’s fascinating. Are you interested in travelling?      
<span style="color:grey;font-size:12px;">是的，这确实很有趣。你对旅行感兴趣吗？</span>

Oh yes! I love travelling. I haven’t got much time with the bar and everything…  but there are so many places I’d like to visit        
<span style="color:grey;font-size:12px;">哦，是的！我喜欢旅行。因为酒吧和其他事情，我没有太多时间……但是有很多地方我想去参观</span>

Do you travel a lot     
<span style="color:grey;font-size:12px;">你经常旅行吗</span>

Yes, I do     
<span style="color:grey;font-size:12px;">是的，我经常旅行</span>

Lucky you. Have you got a lot of free time?       
<span style="color:grey;font-size:12px;">你真幸运。你有很多空闲时间吗</span>

Not really, I travel for my job.     
<span style="color:grey;font-size:12px;">并非如此，我旅行是因工作需要。</span>

Oh wonderful!     
<span style="color:grey;font-size:12px;">哦，太棒了！</span>

What do you do ?     
<span style="color:grey;font-size:12px;">你是做什么工作的？</span>

I’m a photographer     
<span style="color:grey;font-size:12px;">我是一名摄影师</span>

Are you ? How interesting!  Is this one of your pictures then ?     
<span style="color:grey;font-size:12px;">你是吗？真有趣！那这是你的画作之一吗？</span>

No, it isn’t. But I know the photographer who took it     
<span style="color:grey;font-size:12px;">不，不是。但我认识拍这张照片的摄影师</span>

Do you work for a magazine?      
<span style="color:grey;font-size:12px;">你为杂志工作吗？</span>

Sometimes I work freelance.So, I work for different magazines and newspapers. But I also work for a publishing company. I take pictures for their travel books and guides    
<span style="color:grey;font-size:12px;">有时我做自由职业者。所以，我为不同的杂志和报纸工作。但我也为一家出版公司工作。我为他们的旅行书和指南拍照</span>

Wow! That sounds really interesting !  You probably like travelling too, then?       
<span style="color:grey;font-size:12px;">哇！那听起来真的很有趣！那你很可能也应该喜欢旅行吧？</span>

Yes, very much. In fact, I’m not often in London, I spend most of my time travelling! I’ve got a job to do in Chile. Some pictures for a magazine. I’m going to be there for a few months actually     
<span style="color:grey;font-size:12px;">是的，非常喜欢。事实上，我不常在伦敦，我大部分时间都在旅行！我在智利有一份工作。为杂志拍一些照片。实际上我要在那里待几个月</span>

Gosh! How I envy you!     
<span style="color:grey;font-size:12px;">天哪！我真羡慕你啊！</span>

Do you want to come with me ?      
<span style="color:grey;font-size:12px;">你想和我一起去吗？</span>

I wish!     
<span style="color:grey;font-size:12px;">我希望如此！</span>

So, why do you like travelling so much?     
<span style="color:grey;font-size:12px;">那么，你为什么这么喜欢旅行？</span>


Oh for a million reasons! First of all because it’s fun and interesting to see the way people live in other countries. And I think it’s important to have new experiences to expose yourself to other cultures…     
<span style="color:grey;font-size:12px;">有太多原因了。第一条看到其他国家人民的生活方式真是既有趣又令人好奇。而且我认为，拥有新的经历并接触其他文化是非常重要的</span>

Absolutely. I agree. How much do I owe you for the tea?       
<span style="color:grey;font-size:12px;">当然。我同意。我应该给您付多少钱的茶钱呢？</span>

Oh, nothing. It’s on the house     
<span style="color:grey;font-size:12px;">哦，没什么。这顿饭我来买单。</span>

Well, thanks…      
<span style="color:grey;font-size:12px;">嗯，谢谢……</span>

My name is Archie. Pleased to meet you      
<span style="color:grey;font-size:12px;">我叫阿尔奇。很高兴见到你</span>

Pleased to meet you, Archie      
<span style="color:grey;font-size:12px;">很高兴见到你，阿尔奇</span>


## <a id="content2">场景二</a>

Is that for me?     
<span style="color:grey;font-size:12px;">那是给我的吗？</span>

No, it’s for mum. She’s on her way here. She usually comes in for a drink after work on Fridays.      
<span style="color:grey;font-size:12px;">不，是给妈妈的。她正在来的路上。她通常星期五下班后来这里喝一杯。</span>

Oh, what day is it today?     
<span style="color:grey;font-size:12px;">哦，今天是星期几？</span>

Friday    
<span style="color:grey;font-size:12px;">星期五</span>

No, it isn’t. It’s Thursday!     
<span style="color:grey;font-size:12px;">不，不是。是星期四！</span>

Ho, yeah. Well, she sometimes comes in on Thursday, too.    
<span style="color:grey;font-size:12px;">哈，是的。好吧，她有时星期四也来。</span>

Really? So today isn’t a special occasion…      
<span style="color:grey;font-size:12px;">真的吗？所以今天不是什么特别的日子……</span>

I don’t think so    
<span style="color:grey;font-size:12px;">我认为不是</span>

What month is it ?     
<span style="color:grey;font-size:12px;">现在是几月？</span>

November.  And voilà ! You know Lucy, the Martini is more than just a cocktail. It’s an American icon. And the preferred drink of such luminaries as Franklin D. Roosevelt, Jack London and Ernest Hemingway. It celebrates the American Dream …      
<span style="color:grey;font-size:12px;">11月。瞧！露西，马提尼不仅仅是鸡尾酒。它是美国的象征。以及富兰克林·d·罗斯福、杰克·伦敦和欧内斯特·海明威等名人的首选饮料。它庆祝的是美国梦……</span>

Yes, and Winston Churchill liked it too. But we’re in the 21st century, it’s November and …     
<span style="color:grey;font-size:12px;">是的，温斯顿·丘吉尔也喜欢。但我们现在是21世纪，现在是11月……</span>

Oh my god. I’m so sorry Lucy! I completely …     
<span style="color:grey;font-size:12px;">哦，天哪。露西，我很抱歉！我完全……</span>

Forgot?    
<span style="color:grey;font-size:12px;">忘了？</span>

Yes…     
<span style="color:grey;font-size:12px;">是的……</span>

Presents! 

Is this for me ?    
<span style="color:grey;font-size:12px;">礼物！这是给我的吗？</span>

Yes, I really am so sorry … Is it already the 20th November?   
<span style="color:grey;font-size:12px;">是的，我真的很抱歉……现在已经是11月20日了吗？</span>

Yes dear. And… as a punishment, you have to wear this hat. And make me a James Bond Martini!    
<span style="color:grey;font-size:12px;">是的亲爱的。作为惩罚。你得戴上这顶帽子。给我来杯詹姆斯·邦德马提尼！</span>

Three parts gin, one part vodka and a touch of dry Kina Lillet.    
<span style="color:grey;font-size:12px;">三份杜松子酒，一份伏特加再加一点干基那利莱。</span>

Shaken, not stirred    
<span style="color:grey;font-size:12px;">摇晃，而不是搅拌</span>

With a twist of lemon!     
<span style="color:grey;font-size:12px;">加了一点柠檬！</span>



## <a id="content3">场景三</a>


I'm so hungry.     
<span style="color:grey;font-size:12px;">我很饿</span>

Well, you're in the right place!     
<span style="color:grey;font-size:12px;">那你来对地方了！</span>

Do you want something sweet or savory?      
<span style="color:grey;font-size:12px;">你想吃甜的还是咸的？</span>    

I'm not sure. Oh, actually No... I don't know.something savory just a sandwich     
<span style="color:grey;font-size:12px;">我不太确定。哦，实际上不……我不知道。咸的就好，来个三明治</span>    

Ok. Ham and cheese? or tuna?     
<span style="color:grey;font-size:12px;">好的。火腿奶酪？还是金枪鱼？</span>  

Um, not Tuna.Ham and cheese sounds good. or maybe something sweet.Oh,I don't know.     
<span style="color:grey;font-size:12px;">嗯，不是金枪鱼。火腿奶酪听起来不错。或者可能是甜的比较好，来个三明治怎么样？</span>   


Are you ok?  
<span style="color:grey;font-size:12px;">你是不是不舒服？</span>  

Yes.Well, I'm a little nervous. I have a date!    
<span style="color:grey;font-size:12px;">是的。嗯，我有点紧张。我有个约会！</span>  

Oh! I see! Anyone I know?    
<span style="color:grey;font-size:12px;">哦！我知道了！我认识的人吗？</span>  

I don't think so. His name's Rick. He's from Ireland.     
<span style="color:grey;font-size:12px;">我想不是。他叫里克。他是爱尔兰人。</span>  

I love Ireland. Irish people are so friendly and fun!    
<span style="color:grey;font-size:12px;">我爱爱尔兰。爱尔兰人非常友好和有趣！</span>  

Well, I like him. So, do you want something sweet to pep you up?     
<span style="color:grey;font-size:12px;">嗯，我喜欢他。所以，你想吃甜的来提神吗？</span>  

Oh no. I haven't got time,Em. I have to meet him in 20 minutes. Oh god, help.     
<span style="color:grey;font-size:12px;">哦，不。我没有时间，艾米。我必须在20分钟后见到他。哦，天哪。</span>  

Calm down. You can't go on an empty stomach. Let me make you a quick snack to take away. And you can eat it on your way there.        
<span style="color:grey;font-size:12px;">冷静点。你不能空腹去约会。让我给你做一个快速小吃带走。你可以在去那里的路上边走边吃。</span>  

Ok, yes you're right.       
<span style="color:grey;font-size:12px;">好的，你说得对。</span>  

So..tell me. What's he like? Handsome?   
<span style="color:grey;font-size:12px;">所以……告诉我。他怎么样？帅吗？</span>  

Very.. He's tall, average build. He's got dark hair and green eyes. He's very shy and extremely creative. He's artist.    
<span style="color:grey;font-size:12px;">非常……他很高，中等身材。他有深色的头发和绿色的眼睛。他非常害羞而且非常有创造力。他是艺术家。</span>  

Sounds perfect.   
<span style="color:grey;font-size:12px;">听起来很完美。</span>  

He is.. going away?    
<span style="color:grey;font-size:12px;">他……要走了？</span>  


Oh, I wish. I need a holiday just to get away from it all. I'm tired of London. I'd like to go on a trip somewhere.                  
<span style="color:grey;font-size:12px;">哦，我希望。我需要一个假期来摆脱这一切。我厌倦了伦敦。我想去某个地方旅行。</span>    

To the seaside?    
<span style="color:grey;font-size:12px;">去海边？</span>  

Yes, a seaside holiday on a beautiful deserted beach. And sleep under the stars... or even to the mountains and spend a week camping, out in the wild...    
<span style="color:grey;font-size:12px;">是的，在美丽的荒凉海滩上度假。躺在星星下……或者甚至去山区度过一周的露营生活，在大自然中……</span>  


A trekking holiday? That's not very relaxing.     
<span style="color:grey;font-size:12px;">徒步旅行度假？这不太放松吧。</span>  


But it's different. You don't have to take the bus and the tube and walk through busy streets. Even a sightseeing holiday. sounds wonderful right now.  Anything just not here. Your sandwich is nearly ready. Would you like a quick glass of wine?     
<span style="color:grey;font-size:12px;">但是它很不同。你不需要坐公交车和地铁，穿过繁忙的街道。甚至观光度假听起来现在就很棒。任何地方都行，只要不是这里。你的三明治快好了。你想喝一杯酒吗？</span>  

A glass of wine. Just what I need.    
<span style="color:grey;font-size:12px;">一杯酒。正是我需要的。</span>  

A toast to your date.    
<span style="color:grey;font-size:12px;">为你的约会干杯。</span>  

And to your holiday.    
<span style="color:grey;font-size:12px;">也祝你度假愉快。</span>  

cheers.    
<span style="color:grey;font-size:12px;">干杯。</span>
